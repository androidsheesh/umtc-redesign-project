Campuses

University Of Mindanao Bansalan

About UM Bansalan

UM Bansalan College is located at the center of the Municipality of Bansalan, Davao del Sur. Founded in 1962, it has grown from a small two-room building into a major educational hub in the region.

University of Mindanao Digos

About UM Digos

Established in 1949, UM Digos College is the first branch of the University of Mindanao. It offers a variety of programs including Business, Arts, and Sciences.

University of Mindanao Panabo

About UM Panabo

Established in 1951, UM Panabo provides affordable, ISO 9001:2015 certified education to the residents of Davao del Norte.

University of Mindanao Peñaplata

About UM Peñaplata

Located in the Island Garden City of Samal, UMPC offers accessible education with a focus on Technical-Vocational-Livelihood programs.

University of Mindanao Tagum

About UM Tagum

UM Tagum College is a major campus providing high-quality education in the heart of Tagum City, serving thousands of students in the Davao Region.
