UNIVERSITY OF

VINCIT OMNIA VERITAS


The largest private, non-sectarian university in Mindanao, Philippines,
founded in 1946 and based in Davao City. It is known for providing accessible,
affordable, quality education, featuring numerous CHED-accredited,
high-performing programs (Centers of Excellence and Development) and a wide branch network.


VISION

The University of Mindanao envisions to be a leading globally engaged university
creating sustainable impact in society.

MISSION

To provide a dynamic,, and supportive, non-sectarian learning environment
that offers high-standard instruction, research, extension, and production.
It is committed to democratizing access to quality, affordable education,
fostering holistic development, and creating a sustainable impact in society.
